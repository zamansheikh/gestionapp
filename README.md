## 📱 About the App

**GestiónApp** is designed for vacation property owners who want to simplify rental management without losing control. With our years of experience and client-focused approach, we built this app to address real-world problems in the vacation rental space.

### 🌟 Key Features
- 📊 **Property Management Dashboard**  
  View and manage bookings, availability, and inquiries.

- 🤝 **Client-Centric Service Plans**  
  Choose from 3 customized service models tailored to your needs.

- 💸 **Performance-Based Pricing**  
  We only charge when you earn — no fixed rates, no hidden fees.

- 📞 **Easy Communication**  
  Reach us directly through WhatsApp, email, or calls.

- 🔒 **No Obligations or Exclusivity**  
  Cancel any time, no strings attached.

## 🔧 Tech Stack
